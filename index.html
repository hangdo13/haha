SP8 - Volunteer Manager

Course number: CS4850 Section 02 Fall Semester 2024

Professor: Sharon Perry

Non-profit organizations utilize volunteers to get their work done. Volunteers generally sign up to help with specific types of tasks for specific periods of time. If a volunteer has to be out, and misses a scheduled task time slot, it creates an urgent need for the Volunteer Manager to replace that volunteer on that particular task. The Volunteer Manager web application facilitates organizing and managing these volunteers and finding a replacement volunteer when the need arises.
